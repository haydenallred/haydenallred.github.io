Fun For Some or Fun For None?

by Haydey Poo

Look, I'm no seafood guy, but I am so happy that others are able to receive enjoyment from eating such garbage. I'm more of a steak fella. Instead of eating something that was only swimming around five minutes ago, or an octopus that is still squirming and suction-cup'ing (sure, that's a verb), I like my meat grilled to a medium perfection and served with fries and mashed potatoes.

That being said, I enjoyed my recent trip to Fish Haven, where all the locals were bananas (still better than seafood) enough to be out in the cold (by choice) ice fishing. What happens if you fall in? Is that part of the thrill? I just don't get it... I'm turning on Disney+.
