Don't Even Try It...

by Haydey Poo

I like my beverages to be freezing cold. I place around 4-8 ice cubes in my drink each time, and wait a few minutes until it's become so cold that it could give you a brainfreeze. I was prepared for this during my trip to Soda Springs, until I learned that not only was the soda in Soda Springs not actually drinkable, but that it was so blistering hot it would burn you!

I can't even begin to describe how cheated and lied to I feel. And to think I thought my tax dollars were going towards a geyser full of Coca Cola.
