Don't Take Me Too Seriously

by Haydey Poo

Welcome to the inner machinations of my mind- they are quite the enigma. Heads up: don't take me too seriously or you'll probably drown in Fish Haven because I jokingly told you to take a swim. In a lighthearted and frantic manner, I present to you my weather website, "Freaking Snow".

Themed to Idaho's holy grail of cinema, Napoleon Dynamite (which I don't actually enjoy), Freaking Snow provides a comic update on the weather around you. And, when you start to feel bad for yourself, it reminds you that you, in fact, chose to live here. I feel no pity for you... gosh!
